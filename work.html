What Is Yield in Studying?

Yield means getting the most results for your effort. Focus on high-yield study methods: active recall, spaced repetition, and practice testing.

High-Yield Study Habits

  • Active recall: Quiz yourself instead of just rereading notes.
  • Spaced repetition: Review material over days/weeks, not all at once.
  • Practice testing: Do old exams or make your own questions.
  • Pomodoro technique: Study in focused 25-min blocks with short breaks.
